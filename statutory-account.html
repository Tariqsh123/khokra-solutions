Management Accounts & Payroll Services

The most important part of our compliance services is the preparation of periodic management accounts. Monitoring and controlling the financial performance of your business is of paramount importance and regular management information is vital to the continued success of your business.

We can either carry out this function for you or help you set up internal systems to generate the information. We also take this service one step further by interpreting these figures for you and making suggestions to help you improve your profits and grow your business.

Running your own payroll can be time consuming and complicated, particularly with the ever-increasing complexity of taxation and employment legislation. There is an increasing burden on businesses to comply with these issues and non-compliance can lead to substantial penalties.

We can prepare payrolls for businesses with 2 to 200 staff. Our confidential and comprehensive service includes:

  • Computer generated payslips
  • Administration of PAYE, National Insurance, etc.
  • Completion of statutory forms (including year-end returns)
  • Summaries and analysis of staff costs

Outsourcing your payroll is a strategic decision. Ask yourself – Is your company able to do the calculations and deal with payroll details with almost 100% accuracy, timeliness, and compliance with tax, law, and government legislation?

Even if you only have a few employees, you will still make savings by engaging us to administer your payroll.
