Payroll Services

Let AF Solutions manage your payroll efficiently so you can focus on running your business. Payroll is a crucial function that ensures employees are paid accurately and on time, while also meeting all HMRC reporting requirements through Real Time Information (RTI) submissions.

Payroll regulations can be complex, and non-compliance may result in penalties, especially with RTI rules and Auto Enrolment requirements. Errors in payroll processing can also lead to overpayments, disputes, or legal issues with employees.

RTI has significantly changed the PAYE system, requiring employers and pension providers to report payments to HMRC at the time they are made. Every payroll cycle must now be submitted electronically without delay.

Our payroll service includes calculation of employer National Insurance contributions, preparation of monthly PAYE returns, and secure delivery of digital payslips via email for easy access and distribution.

We also manage all employee documentation, including issuing P45 forms when staff leave and generating annual P60 statements that summarize total taxable earnings. Our service is fully comprehensive and designed to ensure complete compliance and accuracy.
