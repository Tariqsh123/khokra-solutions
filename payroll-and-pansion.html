Payroll Services

Let AF Solutions Accountants handle your payroll, this can save you valuable time. Payroll is a vital part of a business, making sure employees are paid on time and at the same time ensuring Real Time Information (RTI submissions) are made to HMRC.

The rules regarding payroll can be complex and non-compliance can lead to substantial fines, especially with Real Time Information and Auto Enrolment. You could also overpay or face employee tribunals if payroll is not carried out correctly.

Real Time Information (RTI submission) is the most significant change ever made to PAYE. This means employers and pension providers must tell HMRC about PAYE payments at the time they are made. So every time an employee is paid, the details need to be sent to HMRC electronically.

As part of our payroll services, we calculate your employer’s National Insurance contributions and prepare your monthly PAYE returns. Employee wage slips are sent via email (e-wage slips), allowing you to print them at your business premises or forward electronic versions directly to employees.

AF Solutions also produces P45 forms for employees when they leave, and at the end of the year we generate a P60 for each employee showing all taxable income for the year. We offer a fully inclusive PAYE service.
